Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 100g Butter
  • 1.5l Milk
  • 100g Flour
  • 500g Lasagna pasta sheets
  • 300g Mozzarella cheese
  • 1kg Minced meat
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 200g Spinach
  • 400cc Tomato sauce
  • 300g Cooked ham
  • 200g Grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup White wine
  • 1tbsp Nutmeg

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat the milk with the flour. Mix well until it dissolves completely.
  2. Add the butter, salt, white pepper, and grated nutmeg.
  3. Stir constantly until it thickens.
  4. Chop the onion, grate the garlic clove. Sauté in a saucepan with olive oil.
  5. Add the minced meat, sear it until it's cooked.
  6. Deglaze with white wine.
  7. Place a layer of tomato sauce at the bottom of the baking dish.
  8. Alternate layers of pasta, meat, tomato sauce, béchamel sauce, spinach, ham, and mozzarella.
  9. Finish with a layer of béchamel sauce, generously sprinkled with grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Bake at 180°C for 10 minutes to gratinate and finish cooking.
